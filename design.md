# 🎨 Design Document
## 🏷️ Project: Wedding Venue Finder

---

# 1. 📌 Design Overview

## 1.1 Objective
Design a modern, elegant, and user-friendly interface for a Wedding Venue Finder platform that allows users to easily search, explore, and request bookings for venues.

## 1.2 Design Goals
- Simple and intuitive navigation
- Visually appealing (wedding theme)
- Fast and responsive UI
- Clear call-to-action (CTA)
- Mobile-first approach

---

# 2. 🎯 Design Principles

- Minimal and clean layout
- High whitespace usage
- Emotional and premium feel
- Consistent design system
- Accessibility and usability focus

---
